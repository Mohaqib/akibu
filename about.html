about child care

Child care is a non-charity organization with a mission of sending back every out-of-school child back to school via the IT(information technology) way, this innovation has the mission of bringing the rich and poor closer, (we are the bridge, we are voice of the voiceless) we move village to village, house to house, town to town, city to city, ruga to ruga in search of out-of-school child. we are everwhere because of internet. More than 4.5 billion people now use the internet, nearly 60 percent of the world population is already online. So we believe 60 percent of world population(4.5 billion people) are our agents. The children of today are gearing up to become adult citizens of tomorrow. It is widely accepted that the learning process is instrumental in shaping one's personality and the way he/she deals with the situation of life. The shift of thoughts from bookish knowledge to knowledge of life, in schools, has brought forth a sea of change. People have warmed up to the idea of education being the key to a well-rounded development instead of just a mean to acquire degrees and monetary success in life. Education must facilitate the cultivation of healthy thought process and groom our cognitive abilities. In the present competitve world, education is abasic necessity for human beings after food, clothes and shelter. Education forms the foundation of any society. It is responsible for the economic, social, and political growth and development of society in general. The thread of the growth depends upon the quality of education that is being imparted. So schools plays an important role in moulding a nation's future by faciliting all round development of its future citizen. The best a parent can give is a sound education.
